About IDR

Industry-Led.
Academically Grounded.

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber and technology risk. We exist to close the critical skills gap in regulated, high-consequence environments — from financial services to critical national infrastructure.

Our model uniquely combines academic insight through a UK university partnership with hands-on, project-based industry practice. Practitioners leave IDR not just qualified, but truly capable — ready to design, implement and lead digital risk functions from day one.

We don't just train. We certify, deploy and innovate — building a pipeline of talent and IP that keeps pace with an evolving threat landscape.

Academic Partnership
Structured curriculum co-developed with a leading UK university, ensuring academic credibility alongside real-world applicability.
Regulated Environments
Specialist focus on financial services, critical infrastructure, and other high-consequence sectors where digital risk is mission-critical.
End-to-End Deployment
From training through to talent deployment — IDR stays engaged through every stage of a practitioner's journey into the industry.

Service Model

Three Pillars.
One Mission.

01
Academy
Structured training programs and bootcamps for students and working professionals. From foundational digital risk literacy to advanced cyber risk specialisations aligned to real employer needs.
02
Innovation & Incubation
Developing proprietary IP, next-generation risk models and AI governance frameworks. IDR incubates ideas at the intersection of academic research and industry need — from concept to deployable solution.
03
Advisory Services
Helping organisations implement globally recognised risk frameworks including NIST, ISO 27001 and NIS2. Practical, embedded advisory that builds internal capability, not dependency.
